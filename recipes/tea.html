Tea

Description

Black tea is a simple refreshing drink made using tea powder, water without using milk. Black Tea can be prepared using sweetner or without it too.

Ingredients

  • Tea. Can be either tea bags or tea powder
  • boiling water

Steps

  1. Boil water well till it starts to boil rigourously
  2. Add tea. Let it boil for 2-3 mins, switch off heat source.
  3. Keep closed for 2 mins for the flavours to steep in.
  4. Serve in a cup and add sugar to taste while stirring
